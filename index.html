Stronghold Location Calculator

Enter the x and z coordinates, and the yaw angle of any two eye of ender throws. This calculator will then give you the location of the stronghold. Due to how minecraft rounds the angle, having the two throws atleast 200 blocks apart will make it more accurate. Also, since strongholds generate only within approximately 25,000 blocks from 0,0 being within that region is recommended for best results.
